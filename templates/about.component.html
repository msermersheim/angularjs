About Us

For more than 20 years, Power BackUPSolutions has worked with resellers nationwide to provide the highest quality UPS solutions. While the company's focus has long been working with IBM business partners, Power BackUPSolutions also takes pride in serving other VAR organizations across the U.S. At the helm of Power BackUPSolutions is Mike Sermersheim, a seasoned sales professional with more than 30 years of experience in sales and management with IBM, United Technologies, and Bell and Howell.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide professional power backup solutions for large and small data centers. Designed to meet both the current and future requirements of our customers, the solutions we supply are built to last, competitively priced, and include excellent customer service long after the installation is complete.
